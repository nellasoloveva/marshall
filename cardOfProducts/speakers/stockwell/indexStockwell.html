STOCKWELL
MARSHALL SIGNATURE SOUND
Stockwell II has three class D amplifiers to power its subwoofer, front and rear facing tweeters, for a sound that’s brilliantly powerful and clear. The cabinet is constructed with a bass reflex system that improves bass efficiency and extends the frequency response while lowering distortion.
TRUE STEREOPHONIC (360° SOUND)
Utilising True Stereophonic, Stockwell II provides a multi-directional experience that will immerse you in your music, indoors or out. Experience absolute 360° sound where every spot is a sweet spot.
ICONIC & RUGGED DESIGN
Echoing the legacy of rock ‘n’ roll, Stockwell II has a solid metal grille that pays homage to the steel mesh grille found on many microphones. It also comes equipped with analogue control knobs to fine-tune your music. Stockwell II’s roadworthy construction with silicone exterior also makes it extremely rugged and durable.
20+ HOURS OF PORTABLE PLAYTIME
Stockwell II has quick charging capabilities so you can pick up and leave at a moment’s notice. 20 minutes gets you 6 hours of portable playtime, while a fully charged battery can be reached in 5 hours. Take it with you wherever the long road leads.
WATER-RESISTANT (IPX4)
Stockwell II has an IPX4 water-resistance rating, so it’s able to withstand splashes of water when the weather starts to turn. It’s the perfect speaker to take with you, even when the elements outdoors aren’t perfect.
CARRYING STRAP
Every moment of your life deserves its own soundtrack. Featuring a guitar-inspired carrying strap, Stockwell II is the perfect music companion to take with you. Whether you're heading to the beach or a backyard BBQ, make your listening session one to remember with Stockwell II.

MINOR III
Marshall signature sound
Minor III brings you unrivalled Marshall signature sound without any extra clutter. Every frequency gets its time to shine so that you never have to compromise on quality. Let that legendary Marshall sound take centre stage.
CUSTOM TUNED DRIVERS
Minor III will blow you away with 12 mm drivers, custom-tuned to deliver enhanced bass, smooth mids and clear highs. Minor III lets you experience sound the way Marshall intended.
ICONIC MARSHALL DESIGN
If something’s worth listening to, it’s worth playing on something that knows how to make an entrance. Thanks to its compact design, Minor III will be a good fit for most users. Make a statement with Minor III in Black, Cream or Burgundy.
25 HOURS OF WIRELESS PLAYTIME
You don’t want to be left stranded without music when you need it most, so Minor III packs 25 total hours of wireless playtime to keep you moving. The earbuds hold 5 hours and the portable charging case carries four additional charges so you can slip the earbuds into the case for some extra power when needed.
Wireless charging
You can easily charge the case wirelessly or with a USB-C cable. These wireless earbuds do all the heavy lifting for you, so all you have to do is grab your music and go.
INTUITIVE TOUCH CONTROLS
Minor III comes with mirrored touch-sensitive earbuds, so you can use either one to control your music and phone calls. Removing and inserting an earbud also initiates the auto-pause and -play feature, so you won’t miss a single beat.
